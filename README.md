# Google Play Store Apps Analysis (EDA)

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Google Play Store Apps dataset. The objective is to identify factors that contribute to app success, understand user behavior, analyze category performance, and generate business insights that can support app development and marketing decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

The Google Play Store contains thousands of applications across multiple categories. Understanding what drives app ratings, installs, and user engagement can help organizations build better products and improve business outcomes.

This analysis focuses on identifying:

* Factors contributing to app success
* Popular app categories
* Relationship between ratings, installs, and reviews
* Impact of app size and price on user behavior
* User preferences across different categories

---

## Dataset Information

**Dataset Source:**

Google Play Store Apps Dataset

Dataset contains information such as:

* App Name
* Category
* Rating
* Reviews
* Size
* Installs
* Type (Free/Paid)
* Price
* Content Rating
* Genres

---

## Key Findings

* Most applications maintain ratings above 4.0.
* Free applications dominate the Google Play Store ecosystem.
* Communication, Social, and Tools categories attract the highest number of installs.
* Reviews and installs show a strong positive relationship.
* App price has limited influence on user ratings.
* Regularly updated apps tend to remain competitive.
* User engagement is a major indicator of app success.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus development efforts on high-performing categories.
* Encourage user reviews and feedback.
* Maintain frequent app updates.
* Optimize app size for better accessibility.
* Consider freemium business models to increase adoption.

---

## Conclusion

The analysis demonstrates that app success is strongly influenced by user engagement, category selection, ratings, and update frequency. The findings provide actionable insights that can help developers, product managers, and business teams make informed decisions for future app development strategies.
